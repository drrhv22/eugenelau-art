In the studio

I am a painter working in oil and watercolor in San Francisco.

For years I have painted the light of Northern California — its golden hills and coastal fog, a lone tree on a ridge, the long shadows of dusk, a bowl of pears on a windowsill. I work close to home, from life and from memory.

Each canvas is an attempt to hold a passing hour of light — a unique original, painted once and never repeated.
